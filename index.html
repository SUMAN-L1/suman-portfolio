About Me

A researcher rooted in
real-world impact.

I'm Suman L., a Ph.D. Scholar in Agricultural Economics at the University of Agricultural Sciences, Bengaluru. My research focuses on unravelling market dynamics — particularly tomato price forecasting and market intelligence — to support informed decision-making in Indian agriculture.

My journey began in Sericulture, evolved through a rigorous Master's that assessed the economic impact of treated sewage water for irrigation, and has now grown into a full-spectrum research and teaching career powered by data science, GIS, and econometrics.

Beyond research, I actively contribute as a Teaching Assistant, Master Trainer, and Resource Person for advanced statistical and data analytics programmes across Karnataka.

I believe in open knowledge — which is why this page hosts my teaching materials freely for students and fellow researchers.
